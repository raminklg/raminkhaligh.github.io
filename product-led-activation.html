Winning Activation with Product‑Led Growth

Published August 09, 2025

Activation is the hinge metric. If users don’t reach value fast, nothing else matters.

Map the journey

Sign‑up → onboarding → setup → first value. Identify the “aha” moment and quantify it.

Design nudges

  • Contextual prompts inside the product
  • Lifecycle nudges via email/push based on user state
  • Checklists and progress UI

Measure right

Track time‑to‑value, step completion, and 7/14/28‑day retention by cohort.
